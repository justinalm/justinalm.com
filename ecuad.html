Bravado Magazine

2011, I redesigned Bravado Magazine’s brand and website, moving Bravado from a lacklustre Joomla to a WordPress theme of “high gloss pixels”.

—

Objective: Reflect Bravado’s values in their identity, increase presence in a competitive market, unquiely shape content on the web, and ease high-frequency publishing.

Concept, Design, Dev, and PM: Justin Alm

Status: Sunset.
