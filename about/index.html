About Me

Thanks for visiting. I’m currently a User-Experience & User-Interface Designer at Steamclock Software. I live in the world of product strategy, information architecture, and visual design. I care about how things work as much how good things feel.

At Steamclock, my work involves strategy, information architecture, wire-framing, graphic design, and user interface design. Twice a day, I press fine coffees roasted on the West Coast from Vancouver, Victoria, Portland, and San Francisco. If you’re looking for a crack design and dev team for your next product, or to rev one that already exists, we’re the people to talk to. We are experts in iOS, Android, and the Web.

Away from work, I spent time at wife’s shop, Woo To See You Boutique. When we’re not there you can find us walking our favourite routes through Vancouver, eating at our favourite sushi restaurants, cooking up our favorite meals, inspired by art exhibitions, and connecting with family and friends.

You can connect with me via Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and email.
